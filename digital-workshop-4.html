HR & TECHNOLOGY

Students will dive into the fundamentals of leadership and how technology enhances productivity.

Welcome to the Human Resources and Technology workshop! You can find everything you need for this workshop on this page.

There are 6 activities included in this workshop, plus the Learning Challenge.

Let's get creative, ask questions, and be bold!

For Google Drive users, to access your downloaded files:

Open an activity link and make a copy of the activity by going to File > Make a Copy. Then save it onto your own personal Google Drive.

For Microsoft App users, to access your downloaded files:

On desktop/computer: Go to your File Explorer > Downloads. On iPad, Go to your home screen > My Files > Recents

2. Small-Group Activity: Technology

In this activity, you'll learn more about up-and-coming technologies!

1. In small groups of 3-4, make a copy of this JamBoard file.

2. Collaborate to learn more about some cutting-edge technologies. Your options are: Artificial Intelligence, Self-Driving Cars, Automation, and Cryptocurrency. Choose two of these options.

3. Use your online research skills to learn more about these technologies. Summarize your learnings by adding 3-4 key facts to the JamBoard.
